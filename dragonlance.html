Dragon Lance

Description

Dragon Lance is a cost-efficient item that provides bonus attack range and stats. It is especially powerful on ranged agility heroes who rely on positioning and sustained damage output.

When to Use

  • On ranged carry heroes to increase attack range and safety.
  • When you need early stats to survive and deal consistent damage in fights.
  • Can be disassembled later to build Hurricane Pike.
