Heart of Tarrasque

Description

Heart of Tarrasque is a core tank item that dramatically boosts health and health regeneration. It allows heroes to stay longer in fights and recover quickly after disengaging.

When to Use

  • Great for strength heroes or frontliners who take a lot of damage in fights.
  • Ideal when you need to outlast the enemy in extended engagements.
  • Useful for heroes that don't rely on frequent spell usage, as regen stops in combat for ranged users.
