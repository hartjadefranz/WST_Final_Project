Scythe of Vyse

Description

The Scythe of Vyse, also known as Sheepstick, provides a strong disable in the form of a hex. It also grants mana regeneration and intelligence, making it a powerful item for spellcasters like Invoker.

When to Use

  • To disable high-priority targets before they can use spells or escape.
  • When your team lacks crowd control.
  • For Invoker, it helps in securing kills and surviving dangerous encounters.
