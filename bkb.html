Black King Bar

Description

Black King Bar (BKB) grants strength and damage, but its main strength is the active ability that gives spell immunity for a duration. It's a must-have for heroes who need to stay active in fights without being disabled.

When to Use

  • When facing heavy magic damage or crowd control.
  • To guarantee spell execution or initiation without interruption.
  • To survive teamfights and be a frontline presence.
