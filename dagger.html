Blink Dagger

Description

Blink Dagger is a core mobility item that allows instant repositioning over a short distance. It’s essential for initiating fights, escaping danger, or chasing down fleeing enemies. Using Blink Dagger effectively can make or break a fight, especially for heroes who rely on surprise and precise positioning.

Mechanics

  • Teleports the hero to a target point up to 1200 units away.
  • Cannot be used for 3 seconds after taking player damage.
  • No mana cost or stat bonuses.
