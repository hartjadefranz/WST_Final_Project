Invoker - Intelligence Hero

Overview

Invoker is a versatile spell-casting intelligence hero who can invoke 10 different spells using combinations of his three reagents: Quas, Wex, and Exort.

Core Abilities

  • Quas: Grants health regen and powers spells like Cold Snap and Ice Wall.
  • Wex: Boosts movement and attack speed, and is used in spells like EMP and Tornado.
  • Exort: Increases attack damage and enables nukes like Sun Strike and Chaos Meteor.
  • Invoke: Combines any 3 orbs to create one of Invokerâ€™s 10 spells.

Recommended Items

These items enhance Invoker's mana regen, spellcasting, mobility, and teamfight presence. They help you scale into the late game while staying survivable and impactful.

Tips & Strategy

  • Learn the spell combos and when to use them: Tornado + EMP, Cold Snap + Sun Strike, etc.
  • Mastering fast orb-switching and spell invoking is key to high-level play.
  • Use Forge Spirits to harass and push lanes early.
  • Position wisely during fights; you're squishy without proper protection.
