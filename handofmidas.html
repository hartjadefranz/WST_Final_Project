Hand of Midas

Description

Hand of Midas provides a boost to attack speed and its active ability converts a non-hero enemy unit into gold and experience. It is a great farming tool for scaling heroes like Invoker who benefit from fast level and item progression.

When to Use

  • When you want to boost gold and experience gain in the early to mid-game.
  • On heroes that need levels for power spikes, like Invoker's spells.
  • To recover from a slow laning phase and scale into late game.
