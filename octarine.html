Octarine Core

Description

Octarine Core provides a large boost to both mana and health, while reducing the cooldown of all abilities and items. It also grants spell lifesteal, helping spellcasters like Invoker sustain themselves during prolonged fights.

When to Use

  • When your hero relies on frequent spell usage and benefits from reduced cooldowns.
  • To improve spell uptime and survivability in long fights.
  • Best built in the mid to late game when mana pool and sustain become critical.
