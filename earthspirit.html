Earth Spirit - Strength Hero

Overview

Earth Spirit is a powerful initiator and utility hero, capable of turning teamfights with his crowd control abilities and mobility tools.

Skills

  • Boulder Smash: Kicks a rock or enemy, stunning and damaging all enemies hit.
  • Rolling Boulder: Rolls toward a direction, damaging and slowing enemies.
  • Geomagnetic Grip: Pulls a rock toward Earth Spirit, silencing enemies it passes through.
  • Magnetize: Applies a damage-over-time effect that spreads through stone remnants.

  • Prioritize silencing high-impact enemy casters with Geomagnetic Grip.
