Ethereal Blade

Description

Ethereal Blade grants agility, strength, and a powerful active ability: it turns the target ethereal (unable to attack or be attacked physically) and amplifies magical damage taken. Often used to combo with nukes.

When to Use

  • Use on enemies before casting magic spells to deal burst damage.
  • Can also be used defensively to save allies from physical attacks.
  • Popular on heroes with high magic burst like Morphling or Invoker.
