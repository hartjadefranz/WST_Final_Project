Aghanim's Scepter

Description

Aghanim's Scepter upgrades the ultimate or abilities of many heroes, granting them new functionality or enhancing existing spells. It's a versatile item that often changes the hero's power spike significantly.

When to Use

  • When your hero has a strong Aghanim's upgrade that boosts damage, crowd control, or mobility.
  • On heroes that rely heavily on their ultimate or specific core spells.
  • Pairs well with cooldown reduction and mana sustainability items.
