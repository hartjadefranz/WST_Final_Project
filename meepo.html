Meepo - Agility Hero

Overview

Meepo is a highly technical agility hero who specializes in overwhelming enemies with his clones. Perfect for players who can micro-manage effectively.

Skills

  • Earthbind: Throws a net that roots enemies.
  • Poof: Teleports a Meepo clone to another, dealing AoE damage at both ends.
  • Geostrike: Slows enemies and deals damage over time with each attack.
  • Divided We Stand: Creates controllable clones that share experience and items.

Recommended Items

These items improve mobility, survivability, and damage output, allowing Meepo to fight, farm, and pressure lanes more effectively.

Tips & Strategy

  • Practice controlling multiple Meepos efficiently.
  • Push multiple lanes with clone coordination.
  • Avoid fighting without all Meepos grouped.
  • Focus on map awareness and split-pushing.
