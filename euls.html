Eul's Scepter of Divinity

Description

Eul's Scepter gives Earth Spirit movement speed, mana regeneration, and an active cyclone to disable enemies or purge yourself. It's excellent for initiating or saving yourself.

When to Use

  • To interrupt channeling abilities or combos.
  • To self-purge debuffs or escape dangerous situations.
  • To set up skillshots like Rolling Boulder or Boulder Smash.
