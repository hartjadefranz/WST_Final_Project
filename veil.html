Veil of Discord

Description

Veil of Discord is a utility item that amplifies magic damage in an area. It grants useful stats like intelligence and armor while giving Earth Spirit more teamfight presence through spell amplification.

When to Use

  • When your team has strong magic damage to follow up Earth Spirit's initiation.
  • To amplify your own skill damage like Magnetize and Rolling Boulder.
  • Provides helpful early stats and mana regen for sustained fighting.
