Boots of Travel

Description

Boots of Travel allow a hero to teleport to allied units or structures across the map, offering exceptional mobility and map presence. They also significantly increase movement speed.

When to Use

  • When split pushing or responding quickly to enemy aggression is crucial.
  • Great for heroes like Invoker who want to maintain map control and pressure multiple lanes.
  • Useful in the mid to late game for global presence and faster rotations.
