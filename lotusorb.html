Lotus Orb

Description

Lotus Orb grants armor, mana regen, and health regen. Its active ability reflects most targeted spells back to their caster, making it a strong counter to disables and silences.

When to Use

  • To reflect targeted disables and nukes back at enemies.
  • To protect key teammates in fights.
  • To purge debuffs and silences from yourself or allies.
