Scythe of Vyse

Description

Scythe of Vyse, often called “Hex,” is a powerful disable item that turns an enemy into a harmless critter for a few seconds. It also provides a large boost to intelligence, mana regeneration, and overall utility.

When to Use

  • Initiate or counter-initiate on high-value enemy heroes like carries or initiators.
  • Ideal for intelligence heroes who need crowd control.
  • Excellent for breaking Linken’s Sphere due to its instant cast.
